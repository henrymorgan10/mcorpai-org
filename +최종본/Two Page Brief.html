One‑Page Brief for Diplomats

1) Context & Problem

  • In regions where real‑time monitoring is impractical, protection gaps for children with disabilities persist.
  • Server/cloud‑dependent models risk censorship, outages, and recurring cost.
  • Data‑collecting systems face legal and trust barriers (GDPR/child privacy).

2) Our Solution (Serverless · Data‑Free)

  • Offline‑first lightweight AI that runs locally on low‑spec devices.
  • Zero personal data collection (no cookies/logins/analytics/SDKs).
  • Refugee‑assembled, ultra‑low‑cost device + pilot design.
